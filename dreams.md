# Dream Features

These are aspirational features that would make a place truly special. I understand they may be difficult to find, but if a listing has any of these, it's worth highlighting!

## Outdoor Spaces (Top Priority)
- **Rooftop access or rooftop terrace**: Private or shared
- **Garden (tuin)**: Even a small private garden would be amazing
- **Balcony (balkon)**: For morning coffee, plants, fresh air
- **Terrace (terras)**: Ground-level outdoor space
- **Courtyard access**: Shared outdoor communal space

## Cool Outdoor Features
- **Views**: Overlooking parks, canals, interesting architecture
- **Sun exposure**: South or west-facing for afternoon/evening light
- **Privacy**: Outdoor space that's not directly overlooked
- **Outdoor seating area**: Built-in benches, space for a small table
- **Green surroundings**: Trees, plants, nature visible from windows

## Cool Indoor Features
- **Skylights (dakramen)**: Natural light from above
- **Tall ceilings (hoge plafonds)**: 3+ meters, feels spacious and airy
- **Exposed beams or industrial features**: Character and charm
- **Large windows**: Floor-to-ceiling or oversized windows
- **Loft or mezzanine**: Extra vertical space
- **Fireplace**: Even decorative/non-functional adds atmosphere

## Architectural Character
- **Unique layout**: Not a standard box, interesting use of space
- **Original features**: Brick walls, wooden floors, architectural details
- **Modern renovations done tastefully**: Preserves character while updating
- **Corner unit**: More windows, better light
- **Top floor**: More privacy, often more light

## Bonus Dream Scenarios
- **Combination features**: e.g., balcony + skylights, or rooftop + tall ceilings
- **Surprise elements**: Reading nook, window seat, built-in shelving
- **Outdoor + indoor connection**: Large doors opening to balcony/garden
- **Multiple outdoor options**: Both a balcony AND garden access

## Why These Matter
These features transform a functional apartment into a home that inspires and energizes. Natural light, outdoor access, and architectural interest significantly improve quality of life and mental wellbeing. If a listing has even ONE of these dream features, it's worth serious consideration even if other aspects are just "good enough."
